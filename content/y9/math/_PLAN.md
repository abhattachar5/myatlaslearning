## Deferred to YEAR 10 (Higher techniques, not Year 9 foundations)
- **Recurring decimal → fraction by algebra** (AQA N10, Higher). Year 9 only
  classifies terminating vs recurring + simple conversions.
- **Quadratic-sequence nth term** (AQA A25, Higher). Year 9 only recognises and
  continues non-linear sequences; the nth-term technique lands in Year 10
  alongside quadratics.

When Year 10 is mapped, add these two as explicit islands.
