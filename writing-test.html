Writing Challenge

Put your writing skills to the test! You'll be given a writing task that draws on everything you've learned across Writing Strategies. Write your response, then get instant feedback on your work.

Target
200–400 words

Your writing will be assessed on:

  • Planning & Organisation
  • Argumentative Writing
  • Creative Writing Techniques
  • Editing & Proofreading
  • Research & Referencing
  • Transitions & Conjunctive Adverbs

Only the criteria relevant to your prompt will be scored.

Your writing should include:

    0 words

    Evaluating your writing...

    Your work is being read and feedback is being prepared. This usually takes 5–10 seconds.
